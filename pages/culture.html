Cuture Corner

Explore Cirta through art, traditions, and stories! Culture Corner is your gateway to vibrant images, historical insights, and fascinating cultural artifacts.

The Spring Festival in Constantine revives the heritage of flower and rose distillation through folkloric performances and workshops that highlight traditional crafts and promote cultural tourism.

The Constantine gandoura, with its luxurious gold embroidery and traditional design, embodies rich Algerian heritage and stands as a symbol of elegance and craftsmanship.

Jouzia is a traditional sweet of Ottoman origin, famous in Constantine, where families continue to make it especially during Ramadan.

Constantine preserves its copperware heritage and produces the largest share of traditional copper products in Algeria, enhancing its status regionally and internationally.
